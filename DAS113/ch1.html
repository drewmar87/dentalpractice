Module Objectives:
Upon completion of this module, the student should be able to:
Lesson 1.1: History of Dentistry
1. Pronounce, define, and spell the key terms.
2. Summarize the main historical evolution of dental history by its century.
  a. Name the four major ancient civilizations that practiced dentistry.
  b. Describe the role of barbers in dentistry during the renaissance period.
  c. Explain how dentists learned their profession in the 18th century.
  d. Explain the meaning “Birth of a Profession” from the 19th century.
  e. Recall the dental specialties that were formative during the 20th century.
  f. List the advances in dentistry from the 21st century, and what these advances hope to improve in oral health care.
3. Explain why dentistry has lagged behind in recruiting a more divers population.
  a. Describe why women are a good fit for the profession of dentistry.
  b. Discuss how the American Dental Association took leadership in recruiting a more diverse population in dentistry.
4. Explain how the term “allied health” plays into the history of dental assisting and dental hygiene.
  a. Describe how dental assisting came about in the late 1800s.
  b. Explain the role of the dental hygienist from the beginning of its profession.
