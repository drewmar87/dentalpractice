DAS 113 – Day 2 In Person Overview (8/22/23)

Hello Everyone,

Day 2 of DAS 113 and we're exploring into the world of dentistry in DAS 113! Today's focus is on Infection Prevention and Disease Transmission. Let's take a glimpse at our itinerary:

  • Introduction - Begin with the significance of infection control in dentistry. We'll have a compelling presentation highlighting real-world implications of poor infection control.
  • Disease Transmission - We'll learn about the main ways diseases can spread in a dental environment. Stay alert for a group activity on identifying types of disease transmission in dental scenarios.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) - Ever wondered about the right way to wear and remove gloves, masks, and gowns? A comprehensive presentation on PPE awaits you, followed by a hands-on demonstration.
  • Hand Hygiene - An essential element of infection control, we'll discuss the when, why, and how of proper hand hygiene, further deepening your practical skills.
  • Wrap-Up & Look Ahead - Recap of today's key lessons, a quick quiz to consolidate learning, and a Q&A session to clarify any doubts. A preview of our next class will also be provided.

I look forward to seeing everyone's active participation. Let's make Day 2 another great class!

DAS 113 – Day 3 In Person Overview (8/23/23)

Hello Everyone,

Day 3 of DAS 113 and we're diving into the principles and techniques of Disinfection, Instrument Processing and Sterilizing. Here's what we've planned:

  • Classroom Session - A comprehensive theoretical dive including:
    • Introduction - Understanding the importance of disinfection and sterilization in a dental clinic.
    • Principles of Disinfection - Unraveling the types and functions of disinfectants.
    • Techniques of Instrument Processing - Exploring manual cleaning, ultrasonic cleaners, and instrument washers.
    • Sterilizing Techniques - Learning about the role of autoclaves, dry heat sterilizers, and chemical vapor sterilizers.
    • Overview of Infection Control - A primer on the correct use of personal protective equipment and key infection control practices.
  • Clinic Session - A hands-on experience covering:
    • Operatory Setup and Tear Down - Real-life training on operatory setup and tear down, emphasizing on disinfectant use and proper handling of items.
    • Instrument Processing and Sterilizing - A live demonstration of cleaning instruments, autoclave operation and safety precautions.
    • End-of-Day Cleanup and Infection Control - Mastering the post-treatment cleanup and routine maintenance of sterilization equipment.
  • Wrap-Up & Look Ahead - Recap, common mistakes and best practices discussion, a Q&A session, and a preview of our next class.

Looking forward to your active participation as we learn and grow together!
